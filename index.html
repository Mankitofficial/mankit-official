About Me

My name is Mankit, born and raised in Karnal. I completed my schooling from PM Shri Government Senior Secondary School, Karnal, and I am currently a JEE aspirant who believes in structured learning, self-discipline, and long-term consistency.

Every achievement so far stands on the support of my parents, teachers, mentors, and well-wishers who guided me at different stages of my life.
